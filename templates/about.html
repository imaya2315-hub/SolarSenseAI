Why a hybrid model?

Solar output depends on both the current weather snapshot and what happened just before it. The CNN layers pick up on patterns across the input features, while the LSTM layers carry information forward in time — together they capture more than either could alone.

Why explainability matters

A number alone isn't actionable. SolarSense AI runs a perturbation analysis on each input — nudging it slightly and measuring how much the prediction moves — to approximate SHAP-style feature attributions without needing the original training pipeline.
